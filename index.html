Privacy Policy

Unit Utils

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Unit Utils is a simple unit converter app built for everyday use. It runs entirely on your device and is designed with your privacy in mind.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not track your activity, and does not use analytics or advertising services.

Third-Party Services

The app fetches currency exchange rates from a public API (frankfurter.dev) to support currency conversions. This request does not include any personal or device information — it is a simple data request for current exchange rates.

Data Storage

Unit Utils does not store any data on your device or on remote servers. All conversions are calculated locally and nothing is saved between sessions.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data from any user, this includes children. The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
